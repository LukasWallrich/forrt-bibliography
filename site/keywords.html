Classification keywords

These are the keywords the build pipeline uses to decide whether a work counts as "open scholarship" and which FORRT cluster(s) it belongs to. A work is tagged with a cluster when its title or abstract contains any of that cluster's keywords (case-insensitive, word-boundary match). Tweak them in scripts/build_clusters.py — specifically the EXTRA_KEYWORDS dict and ALLOWED_SINGLES set.

Keywords derived from FORRT's Clusters v4.1 document.
