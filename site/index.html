          About this bibliography

          How it's built

          Contributors come from the Tenzing credit sheets that power forrt.org/contributions. For each contributor with an ORCID, we query OpenAlex and classify every work by matching its title and abstract against keyword lists derived from FORRT's Clusters v4.1 taxonomy.

          Caveats

          • Keyword matching is imperfect. Works may appear under multiple clusters or be missed if they use unusual vocabulary. View the keyword list →
          • OpenAlex coverage varies by discipline; some works (esp. book chapters, older conference papers) may be absent.
          • Co-authorship edges are computed on open-scholarship works only, to keep the graph legible.
          • Pre-2000 publication years are not shown in the chart — OpenAlex backfill is unreliable that far back.
